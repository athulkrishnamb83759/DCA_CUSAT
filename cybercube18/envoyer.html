Farewell To Pramod Sir

In recognition to the fruitful service Dr. Pramod has rendered to the Department and the University.

Dr. K V Pramod

Dr. K. V. Pramod joined Cochin University of Science and Technology in April 1990. He is an MSc in Mathematics from University of Kerala and PhD from Cochin University of Science and Technology. Since his joining in the University, he has been a dedicated professional, sincere to his work and employer. He is a gentleman with high social values and has proven a role model for the generation to follow.
Dr. Pramod has served the University in various roles like member of Senate, Member of Academic Committee, Member of Staff Council, Chairman board of Studies in Computer Applications, Member of Faculty of Technology, Member of Academic Council, Member of NSS Advisory Committee , Member of Scrutiny Committee and Head of the Department of Computer Applications. He has also collaborated with other Universities relating to academic matters. Sir has produced many PhDs under him and is presently guiding PhD scholars. He has many National and International publications to his credit and a MODROBS project too. Dr. K. V. Pramod is the pioneer behind the Cyber Intelligence Research Laboratory in the Department of Computer Applications. Above all, Sir has initiated the National Conference series NCILC
